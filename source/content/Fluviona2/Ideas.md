## Bag of Tricks
The _Bag of Tricks_ is a classic. It’s uncommon and doesn’t require attunement, which is already awesome. Its function is quite simple – as an action, you can pull out a random creature from the bag, which is friendly to you. There are a hundred different ways to use a _bag of tricks_, ranging from getting an ally in combat to procuring a minesweeper, creating a diversion, and so on. 

Because the _bag of tricks_ is random, you never know whether you’ll get a **dire wolf** or a **weasel**, and that just makes it that much more fun. All told, the _bag of tricks_ is a great magic item that any character would be happy to have.
